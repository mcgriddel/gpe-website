Investment criteria

Three tests every opportunity must pass before we commit.

01
People

Talented, experienced teams with a proven record of launching, scaling, and successfully exiting companies.

02
Product

Innovative, market-leading products underpinned by defensible IP, proprietary technology, or exclusive data ownership.

03
Potential

How we invest

Disciplined process. Aligned capital. Hands-on partnership.

We invest alongside a select group of leading institutional investors we have known for years — sharing knowledge and diligence to reduce risk for ourselves and our partners.

Every opportunity is assessed against a clear investment mandate before we commit. Once invested, we support founders with operational expertise, board-level guidance, international expansion, and follow-on financing.
