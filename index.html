What we do

We put capital to work in deep technology — and help others do the same.

GPE Partners is a Geneva-based investment and advisory house focused on deep technology. We invest in growth-stage companies alongside leading institutional partners — and we advise investors and founders on accessing, structuring, and raising the capital behind them.

01

Investments

We invest and co-invest in growth-stage deep-technology companies — Series B to pre-IPO — taking growth-equity and private-equity positions alongside leading institutional partners, often through dedicated co-investment syndicates.

02

Advisory

We advise investors on curated access to deep-tech opportunities, and founders on capital raising and M&A — drawing on 25 years and a global private-markets network.
